How It Works

Once you submit your pickup request, our team will:

  1. Confirm your pickup details via email or phone
  2. Arrive at your location on the scheduled date and time
  3. Collect your e-waste items safely
  4. Verify the items and credit points to your account

Pickup Areas

We currently serve the following areas:

  • Metro City Center
  • North District
  • South Suburbs
  • East Valley
  • West Hills

Don't see your area? Contact us to check availability.

Preparation Tips

  • Gather all e-waste items in one accessible location
  • Remove any personal data from devices if possible
  • Place smaller items in a box or bag
  • Ensure items are easily accessible for our team
